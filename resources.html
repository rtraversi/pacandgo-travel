Essential Travel Tips

📋

Book Early for Best Pricing

Cruise and resort prices typically increase as departure dates approach. Booking 6–12 months out often means better cabins, lower prices, and more onboard credit opportunities.

🛂

Check Your Passport

Many countries require your passport to be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel dates. Check expiration dates well in advance — renewals can take 8–11 weeks.

💳

Notify Your Bank

Before traveling internationally, notify your bank and credit card companies of your travel dates and destinations to avoid having your cards flagged for suspicious activity while abroad.

🧳

Pack Light & Smart

Check airline baggage policies before packing. For cruises, formal nights may require dressy attire. Your agent can advise on what to expect for your specific itinerary.

📱

International Phone Plans

Avoid surprise roaming charges. Most major carriers offer temporary international plans. For cruises, ship Wi-Fi packages can also be purchased in advance at a discount.

⏰

Arrive a Day Early

For cruises and international flights, consider arriving at your departure city the night before. Flight delays happen — missing your ship's departure is not covered by most cruise lines.
