Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Last updated: May 5, 2026

PAC and Go Travel ("we," "us," or "our") is a full-service travel agency and host agency affiliated with CLIA (#456611). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or submit a form to any of our travel agents.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you:

  • Submit a trip planning or quote request form
  • Contact a travel agent through an agent profile page
  • Sign up for our newsletter or promotional communications
  • Submit a lead form through a Google Ad or other advertising channel

This information may include your name, email address, phone number, travel preferences, number of travelers, destination interests, travel dates, and budget information.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Connect you with a PAC and Go Travel agent who can assist with your trip
  • Respond to your travel planning and quote requests
  • Provide personalized vacation and cruise recommendations
  • Send you relevant travel information and offers (if you opt in)
  • Improve our website and agent services

Information We Do Not Share

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared with travel suppliers (cruise lines, tour operators, hotels, airlines) only as necessary to book and manage travel on your behalf, and only with your consent.

Agent Communications

When you submit a contact form addressed to a specific travel agent, your inquiry is delivered directly to that agent's email address. Your contact information will be used by that agent solely to respond to your inquiry and assist with your travel planning.

Google Services

Our website may use Google Analytics and Google Ads tracking to understand how visitors use our site and to measure the effectiveness of our advertising. These services may collect anonymized data about your visit. You can opt out of Google Analytics by visiting Google's opt-out page.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and to support advertising measurement. By using our site, you consent to our use of cookies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time.

Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your personal information. Our website is served over HTTPS (SSL encryption). Form submissions are transmitted securely via EmailJS. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time. To make such a request, please contact us at pacandgopatty@gmail.com.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
