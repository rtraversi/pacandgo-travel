What Travel Insurance Protects

✈️

Trip Cancellation

Covered if you need to cancel for a covered reason — illness, injury, job loss, jury duty, and more. Get your money back, not just a credit.

🏥

Medical Emergencies

Your domestic health insurance often doesn't cover you abroad. Travel insurance covers emergency medical care and evacuation wherever you are.

🧳

Lost or Delayed Baggage

Airlines lose bags every day. Travel insurance reimburses you for lost, stolen, or delayed luggage so you're not stuck without essentials.

⛵

Missed Connections

Flight delays, weather, and missed ports happen. Coverage kicks in to cover additional travel costs and catch-up arrangements.

🌪️

Travel Delays

If your trip is delayed 6+ hours, get reimbursed for meals, accommodation, and other out-of-pocket expenses while you wait.

🚑

Emergency Evacuation

Medical evacuations can cost $100,000+. Travel insurance covers transport to the nearest adequate medical facility or home.

Agent Advice

Smart Insurance Tips From Our Team

1

Buy At Time of Booking

Purchase insurance when you make your first trip payment to qualify for pre-existing condition waivers and the widest range of covered reasons.

2

Insure the Full Trip Cost

Cover the total non-refundable cost of your trip including flights, cruise fare, hotels, and pre-paid excursions to maximize your protection.

3

Consider Cancel For Any Reason

CFAR upgrades typically cover 75% of your trip cost and let you cancel for literally any reason — perfect for uncertain travel plans.

4

Check Your Credit Card Coverage

Some premium credit cards offer trip protection, but it's rarely enough on its own. Know what you have — then fill the gaps with a dedicated policy.
